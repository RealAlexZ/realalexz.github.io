My Biography

About Me

Hi! I am Alex.
 An undergraduate student at the University of Michigan pursuing a dual degree in Computer Science and Music, I aspire to be great at the unique confluence where digital logic intertwines with sounds.

I am most passionate about these research areas:
AI/ML for Audio

I am ecstatic about developing novel interfaces that bridge the gap between creativity and execution, potentially integrating human-centered machine learning into these tools. Moreover, I am committed to making AI accessible to everyone, especially those with sensory impairments.

Ethnomusicology

My interest centers on organology, especially traditional Chinese instruments from the Ming and Qing dynasties in a cross-cultural context. Additionally, I am intrigued by virtual instruments and how the digitalization of sounds could influence the future of musical instrument development.
